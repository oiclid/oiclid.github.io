About Me

I am a Software Developer with extensive experience working with Python, JavaScript, Rust, Lisp, R, and WordPress; providing Technical Support; and managing teams. I have built applications using Django, Flask, and Node, as well as worked with various libraries including d3.js, React, Pandas, SciKits, and Matplotlib.

I have over 15 years experience working for startups, design studios, and consultancies. These include 42Labs, PM Global Technology, Center for Health and Development, and Cetra Computer Systems. I have also worked on projects for BudgIT, Dash, Nigerian Universities Commission, Academic Staff Union of Universities, Formelo, Forever Stoked, Evidence Based Asset Management, and a lot more. After spending the better part of the last 6 years working with/for various blockchain projects (including Joystream, Stacks, Polkadot, NEAR), and Akash, I founded, and I'm currently the CEO of, Alien Robot Ninja, a blockchain Infrastructure startup.
